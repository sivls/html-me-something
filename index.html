The Weird and Wonderful World of Dagorhir

Do you enjoy series like Lord of the Rings and Game of Thrones? Do you like to dress up in costume, or even make your own costumes? Does hitting your friends with foam weaponry in mock medieval combat sound like your idea of a good time? If so, you might enjoy Dagorhir, a full-contact foam fighting sport that drops you right in the middle of your favorite fantasy settings.
